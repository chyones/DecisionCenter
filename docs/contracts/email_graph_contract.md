# Email Graph Contract

Email evidence is timeline and responsibility evidence. It is not contractual
authority unless contract terms allow formal notice by email.

## Retrieval

- Search the requesting user mailbox.
- Search authorized shared mailboxes only.
- Apply project, vendor, contract, date, and keyword filters.

## Storage

Store excerpts, message IDs, mailbox, timestamp, and SHA-256 hashes. Do not
store full email bodies in final reports.
